Job Offer Evaluation Tool

Welcome to J.O.E Tool
The tool will deliver trustworthy and transparent evaluations by linking to reliable knowledge sources, such as industry standards or tax implications, ensuring users understand the full scope of each offer. Offers will be ranked from best to worst, based on customizable criteria like overall compensation, growth potential, and work-life balance.
Ultimately, the tool aims to empower job seekers by simplifying complex offer comparisons and enabling them to choose the best opportunity for their career goals.

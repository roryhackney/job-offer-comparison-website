Our product will allow users to compare two or more job offers and access it conveniently through a web browser. It will provide a comprehensive analysis of various compensation types, including salary, benefits, stock options, and bonuses, helping users make informed decisions. Users will also be able to upload job offers in PDF format, making the process of inputting offer details seamless. The tool will deliver trustworthy and transparent evaluations by linking to reliable knowledge sources, such as industry standards or tax implications, ensuring users understand the full scope of each offer. Offers will be ranked from best to worst, based on customizable criteria like overall compensation, growth potential, and work-life balance. Clear, detailed explanations of terms, compensation packages, and their financial impact will help users feel confident in their choices. Ultimately, the tool aims to empower job seekers by simplifying complex offer comparisons and enabling them to choose the best opportunity for their career goals.

About The Team

RREN is a team project focused on creating a comprehensive Job Offer Evaluation Tool, developed by a group of four dedicated Computer Science seniors at North Seattle College, based in Seattle, Washington. Development of the project began in September 2024, with a mission to help individuals make informed decisions when choosing between job offers. This open-source project aims to provide a high-quality, community-driven tool that simplifies complex calculations, empowering users to evaluate job offers with confidence and clarity. We're committed to delivering a reliable, impactful solution that brings value to job seekers everywhere.

The Job Evaluation Tool is a web-based platform designed to help users analyze and compare multiple job offers, focusing on compensation packages such as salary, benefits, stock options, and bonuses. Accessible via any web browser, it allows users to compare offers directly, with transparent evaluations and rankings based on personal preferences. Key features include uploading job offers in PDF format, links to external career coaching and salary comparison tools, and clear explanations of compensation terms to help users better understand their offers. The platform includes a user profile system that saves preferences, supports customizable ranking criteria, and provides insights into taxes, take-home pay, and stock options. It also features charts and graphs for visual comparisons, and allows users to save and print their analysis as a PDF.

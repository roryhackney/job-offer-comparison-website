Job Offer Evaluation Tool

Welcome to Job Offer Evaluation Tool
The tool will deliver trustworthy and transparent evaluations by linking to reliable knowledge sources, such as industry standards or tax implications, ensuring users understand the full scope of each offer. Offers will be ranked from best to worst, based on customizable criteria like overall compensation, growth potential, and work-life balance.
Ultimately, the tool aims to empower job seekers by simplifying complex offer comparisons and enabling them to choose the best opportunity for their career goals.

    Key Features

  • PDF Job Offer Upload: Users can upload job offers in PDF format, and the tool extracts relevant data for analysis.
  • Side-by-Side Comparison: Allows detailed comparison of salary, bonuses, stock options, and additional benefits.
  • Customized Ranking System: Users can set personalized ranking criteria based on what matters most to them (e.g., salary, stock options).
  • Compensation Analysis and Explanations: Provides clear explanations of complex terms (e.g., RSUs, profit-sharing) to demystify compensation.
  • Visual Data Representation: Charts and graphs make it easy to compare compensation components visually.
  • User Profiles and Preferences: Saves user preferences, past offers, and custom ranking criteria, creating a personal evaluation record.
  • Tax and Take-Home Pay Estimation: Integrates tax data to give realistic take-home pay estimates.
  • External Resource Links: Connects to career coaching and salary comparison tools for additional guidance.
  • AI-Driven Insights (Optional): Provides personalized analysis using AI to offer deeper insights and recommendations.
  • Long-Term Data Storage: Offers indefinite storage for all uploaded offers and comparisons for future reference.
  • Export and Print Options: Users can save and print analyses as PDF files for easy sharing and physical reference.
